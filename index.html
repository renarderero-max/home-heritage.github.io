About Our Mission

Home Heritage Philippines is dedicated to celebrating and preserving traditional Filipino houses such as the Bahay Kubo, Bahay na Bato, and ancestral homes across the country. We aim to educate future generations about Filipino architecture, history, and identity.

Filipino Cultural Traditions

The Philippines is rich in history, diversity, and vibrant traditions shaped by indigenous roots and centuries of cultural influences. From colorful festivals to strong family values, Filipino culture reflects unity, faith, and community spirit.
